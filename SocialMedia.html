Humans’ desire to find convenient ways to communicate with each other and maintain their relationships has led to the evolution of social media using digital technologies. Over the past decade, it has been noticed that the usage of social media has changed by individuals. Besides that, there were changes that has been made to social media platforms as well.


Change in Platforms

A lot of social media platforms that were available 10 years ago no longer exist. Such as Vine, where users would create short video clips, and they were usually humorous videos, or what they call “memes”. Also, Yahoo was a popular text messenger platform back then where users were able to communicate with other users. However, not every application that was created in that time frame is dead today, a lot of them are still being used. A great example is Facebook, it was created in 2006 and it has been number one social network platform since then. Another example is Instagram, it was created in 2010 and it is still being used till this day. Although, these platforms have changed, a lot of new features has been added to enhance users’ experience.



Change in Usage


As it has been mentioned in the history of social media, the main purpose was to establish an easy and long-distance connection to communicate with others. However, nowadays, people use social media for marketing purposes, a lot of companies and organizations advertise their products and services using social networks, and it had provided them the expected outcome. Moreover, many individuals use social media as their financial income source, they are usually called “social media influencers”. The way that we use social media today was not like that a decade ago, it was much simpler and not as many people used it back then. Advantages

  • Education:

    A lot of educational topics are published in social media to help others to gain more knowledge. People with expertise share their knowledge in social networks with others who are interested. These professionals can consult, and answer asked questions without any charge.



  • Helps in business growth:

    Social media marketing plays a huge role in the development of a business. Almost every person today uses social media, advertising using social networks will help businesses to spread their services across wide number of audiences, and users would share promotional posts to each other easily.



  • Communication:

    The main objective of social media is to be able to communicate and socialise with others in a fast and convenient way, whether it’s in real-time texting form or emailing. Social media has given users the opportunity to meet new people online and build a relationship with them.



  • Entertainment:

    Many people spend their free time scrolling through different social media applications watching the type of content they enjoy and according to their interest. Texting and talking to friends is also considered fun to some people.



  • Keep up with the news:

    People nowadays rarely read newspapers and articles; they mostly receive the news through social media platforms. There are social media accounts that only publish national and worldwide news, and people follow these accounts seeking the latest updates.



Disadvantages

  • Addiction:

    Some people use social media excessively for a long time on a daily basis which then leads to social media addiction. Because of this addiction, other mental health issues might appear such as: anxiety, depression, isolation, and more.



  • Negative comments:

    Bullying in social media is very common and expected. Where users write hateful comments on others’ posts for no obvious reason.Confident people might handle Cyberbullying appropriately. However, Individuals with low self-esteem or young users might take these comments seriously, which then takes a toll on their mental health.



  • False News:

    Rumours and false news in social media spread incredibly fast among the users through different platforms, especially if it concerns a known individual. The reason why it is easy to spread false news in social media is because most people do not investigate the accuracy of the received information, instead, they continue sharing it with others.



  • Hacking:

    Hackers target user accounts with weak passwords or any other vulnerabilities to steal or get access to their personal information,and they usually demand an amount of money in exchange of the information. A user’s sensitive data are at risk if they are not protected appropriately.



  • Time wastage:

    Most people use social media to pass their free time, but excessive usage is considered as time wastage. It is recommended to use the valuable time to accomplish some school or work requirements.
